Frequently Asked Questions
Is Be The Judge free to play?
Yes. The game is free to download and play. It includes optional ads and optional in-app purchases. Core gameplay is always available without payment.
My progress was lost. Can it be restored?
Progress is saved locally on your device. If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, progress may not transfer automatically. We recommend not clearing app data. Cloud save support may be added in a future update.
How do I remove ads?
You can remove ads via the in-app purchase available in the game menu. This is a one-time purchase and persists across sessions on the same device.
The game crashes or freezes. What should I do?
Try the following steps:

1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure your OS is up to date
4. Reinstall the app (note: this may reset progress)

If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and OS version.
I was charged but didn't receive my purchase.
Go to the in-app purchases section and tap "Restore Purchases." If the issue persists, contact us with your transaction receipt from the App Store or Google Play and we'll resolve it promptly.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by the platform where you made the purchase:

• iOS: reportaproblem.apple.com
• Android: Open Google Play → Order history → Request a refund
How do I delete my data?
Be The Judge does not collect personal data that requires deletion. All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. For questions about data handling, see our Privacy Policy.
I found a bug or want to suggest a feature.
We'd love to hear from you. Email us with a description of the bug or your idea. Include your device and OS version for bug reports — it helps us fix things faster.
